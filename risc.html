Italian Network of Kurdish Studies (RISC)

RISC (Rete Italiana di Studi Curdi) is an Italian network of scholars working on Kurdish politics, society and history. I am a co-organiser of the RISC Study Days and active in building collaborations between Italian and Kurdish universities and research centres.
