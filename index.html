Welcome

I am a political scientist working on civil wars, rebel governance, and post-conflict orders, with sustained research on Kurdistan and Sri Lanka. My current work focuses on food security in conflict-affected settings, building on prior research on political risk and early warning systems.

Research focus

Civil wars and rebel governance; post-conflict political orders; political risk and early warning; food security and conflict. Regional focus on Kurdistan, Sri Lanka, and the wider Middle East.
